In this lesson, we will introduce the selector clash attack, which is one of the reasons behind the hack of the cross-chain bridge Poly Network. In August 2021, the cross-chain bridge contracts of Poly Network on ETH, BSC, and Polygon were hacked, resulting in a loss of up to $611 million ([summary](https://rekt.news/polynetwork-rekt/)). This is the largest blockchain hack of 2021 and the second-largest in history, second only to the Ronin Bridge hack. Original tutorial by the [WTF Academy](https://www.wtf.academy/en).
